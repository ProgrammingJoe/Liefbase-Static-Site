Your hub for managing relief efforts

A communication and collaboration platform for disaster relief and humanitarian relief efforts.

Liefbase is a communication and collaboration platform designed to help disaster relief organizations better manage and share information during relief efforts.

In Development

Filtering

Users can choose what layer or piece of information they would like to see or not see. [reword] Information can be filtered by category, organization, or specific resources or hazards.

Customizable functionality

Users can customize their experience by creating and modifying custom map entity types, creating and modifying map layers, and saving filter presets.

Real time updating

Online maps will automatically update for everyone (necessary?) as users add new information. [reword]

Coming Soon

Shared Work Effort

Rather than having all the relief organizations create their own maps for their own use, all information will be available on a singular map. More information is available for all organizations and hundreds of hours of time that was spent updating each individual map will be saved.

Report Generation

Reports can be generated and printed off to be used in presentations and meetings and to be brought into relief zones that do not have online access.

Legacy Imports

Systems will be put in place that allow organizations to import their already existing map data into Liefbase. This saves time and allows for a more integrated working environment with the Liefbase toolset.

Future Products

Starting with Android, mobile development will begin shortly for data collection and offline map use during relief efforts around the world.
